Summary: Latitude in Relation to Weather

The purpose of this project was to analyze how weather changes in relation to its latitude. To accomplish this, OpenWeatherMapAPI was used to pull data to make a dataset of many cities.

After making the dataset, Matplotlib was used to plot various aspects of the weather vs. latitude. The four variables used were max temperature, humidity, cloudiness, and wind speed. This dashboard shows the source data and the visualizations based on the four variables. The trends that is shown in the visualizations is explained on the individual pages.
